Divided in union

This project consists of two parts. The shell part, which can be installed without the desktop. But the desktop on the other hand depends on the shell part.

The terminal/shell experience

This setup uses zsh as shell and interpreter for some helper scripts. The look and feel of the terminal aim to integrate seamless into the desktop experience. But that will be the focus of another video.

The desktop experience

The desktop consists mainly of i3, polybar and rofi. While, there are many other tools that bring this together, these are the ones you are going to interact most with.

So lets introduce you to these.
